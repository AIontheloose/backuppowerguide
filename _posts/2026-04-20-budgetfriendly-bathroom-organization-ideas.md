Bathroom organization can often be overlooked, but it's crucial for maintaining hygiene and saving space. Whether you're looking to declutter or just make better use of the space in your bathroom, there are many budget-friendly ways to achieve this. Below, we explore some effective techniques and recommend a few products that can help you organize your bathroom without breaking the bank.

## Declutter First

Before diving into specific organizational solutions, it’s essential to start by decluttering. Go through your bathroom items and get rid of anything that no longer serves a purpose or is past its expiration date. This will not only make the space more manageable but also ensure you’re using your storage wisely.

## Install Shelving Units

Shelving units are versatile and can be found in various sizes and styles to fit any budget. They provide ample storage for toiletries, towels, and other bathroom essentials. Consider installing a few shelves under the sink or along the walls of your bathroom. A great option is the [AmazonBasics Shelves](https://www.amazon.com.au/s?k=amazonbasics+shelves&tag=owlno-22), which are durable and come in different sizes.

## Use Under-the-Sink Organizers

Under-the-sink organizers can be a lifesaver for storing cleaning supplies, first aid kits, and extra toilet paper. These organizers often feature multiple compartments to keep items organized and easily accessible. The [Scotchgard Easy-to-Install Under Sink Organizer](https://www.amazon.com.au/s?k=scotchgard+under+sink+organizer&tag=owlno-22) is a popular choice, offering protection against leaks.

## Opt for Over-the-Toilet Storage

If you have enough vertical space, consider an over-the-toilet storage unit. These units can hold towels, extra tissues, or even some decorative items. The [Lifefit Bathroom Organizer](https://www.amazon.com.au/s?k=lifefit+bathroom+organizer&tag=owlno-22) is a versatile option that can be easily installed and adjusted to fit your needs.

## Utilize Drawer Organizers

Drawer organizers are perfect for keeping bathroom essentials like makeup, hair accessories, and small tools organized. They come in various sizes and can be found at most department stores or online retailers. The [Klein Tools 3-Compartment Drawer Organizer](https://www.amazon.com.au/s?k=klein+tools+drawer+organizer&tag=owlno-22) is a sturdy option that will withstand daily use.

## Hang Hooks

Hooks are an excellent way to save space and keep items off the floor. Use hooks for hanging towels, robes, or even hair dryers. The [HANGARO 10-Pack Multi-Purpose Hooks](https://www.amazon.com.au/s?k=hangaro+hooks&tag=owlno-22) are a cost-effective solution that can be easily installed and moved as needed.

## Conclusion

Organizing your bathroom doesn’t have to be expensive or complicated. By decluttering, installing shelves, using under-the-sink organizers, over-the-toilet storage units, drawer organizers, and hanging hooks, you can create a more efficient and organized space without spending too much money. Remember, the key is to use what works best for your specific needs.
