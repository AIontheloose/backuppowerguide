# Portable Generators Buying Guide for Homeowners in Australia

## Introduction
In today's modern world, a reliable backup power source is essential for homeowners. Whether it’s a sudden blackout or an extended outage due to natural disasters, having a portable generator can be a lifesaver. This article will help you understand the key factors to consider when choosing a portable generator and provide some recommendations based on Australian needs.

## Key Factors to Consider

### 1. **Type of Fuel**
Portable generators come in different fuel types: petrol (gasoline), diesel, and propane (LPG). In Australia, most homeowners opt for petrol or LPG due to their widespread availability and ease of use.

- **Petrol Generators:** These are the most common type used by homeowners. They are generally more affordable and easier to find than LPG models.
- **Propane Generators:** Ideal for those who already have an LPG supply at home, as it can be a cost-effective option in the long term. However, they require regular maintenance of the fuel tank.

### 2. **Power Output**
Generators are rated by their power output, usually measured in watts (W) or kilowatts (kW). Ensure that your generator has sufficient capacity to run essential appliances during an outage. For a typical Australian home, a 4000W - 8000W generator should suffice for most needs.

### 3. **Noise Level**
Noise is a significant factor when choosing a portable generator. Some models can be quite loud and disruptive, especially if you need to use them frequently or during quiet times like early morning or late at night.

### 4. **Portability**
The size and weight of the generator will determine how easily you can move it around your property. Smaller generators are easier to transport but may have limited power output. Larger models offer more power but are harder to carry.

### 5. **Safety Features**
Look for safety features such as overheat protection, low-oil shutdown, and automatic shutdown when the unit is running out of fuel. These features can prevent damage and ensure safe operation.
