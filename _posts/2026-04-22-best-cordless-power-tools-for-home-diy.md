## Choosing the Right Cordless Power Tool

When selecting cordless power tools, consider the following factors:

### 1. Battery Technology
Look for tools with advanced battery technology like lithium-ion, which offers longer run times and faster charging speeds compared to older NiCad or NiMH batteries.

### 2. Compatibility
Ensure that your chosen tool is compatible with a multi-tool system if you plan on using multiple devices from the same brand. This can save you money in the long run as you won’t need separate chargers and accessories for each tool.

### 3. Tool Quality and Brand Reputation
Choose tools from reputable brands known for their quality and reliability. Brands like DEWALT, Milwaukee, and Bosch are highly regarded among DIY enthusiasts.
