# Best Easter Activities for Families in Australia

Spring is a time of renewal and fun, especially when it comes to Easter. For Australian families, the long weekend provides an excellent opportunity to engage in exciting and meaningful activities. This guide will help you plan memorable Easter events with your loved ones.

## 1. **Easter Egg Hunts**

Egg hunts are a classic tradition that every family should experience at least once. Organize a large-scale hunt around your backyard or local park, where the whole family can participate in the excitement of finding hidden eggs. To make it more engaging, consider using plastic eggs filled with small treats and surprises.

## 2. **Egg Painting**

After the hunt, why not put those colorful eggs to use for a creative craft activity? Egg painting is a fun and easy way to keep little hands occupied while also creating beautiful decorations for your home.

[Non-Toxic Easter Eggs](https://www.amazon.com.au/s?k=Easter+gifts+Australia&tag=owlno-22)

## 3. **Baking Together**

Nothing warms the heart like a homemade treat, and Easter is the perfect time to bake together as a family. Try making traditional Easter cakes or cookies using recipes passed down through generations.

## 4. **Picnics and Outdoor Activities**

Enjoy the beautiful Australian spring weather by organizing a family picnic or participating in outdoor activities like hiking, cycling, or playing sports at local parks.

## 5. **Easter Storytelling and Crafts**

Read Easter-themed stories together to enhance the festive spirit. Follow up with some simple crafts, such as making paper baskets or cross-stitching, which can be both educational and enjoyable for kids.

## 6. **Easter Egg Decorating Workshops**

If you want to add a bit of flair to your egg decorating, consider signing up for an Easter egg decorating workshop. Many community centers and craft stores offer such activities, providing everything you need to create stunning designs.

## 7. **Family Fun Day at the Beach**

End your Easter weekend with a visit to the beach. Pack some beach balls, frisbees, and snorkeling gear for an afternoon of fun in the sun.

## Conclusion

Easter is not just about eggs and bunnies; it's a celebration of family togetherness and cherished traditions. Whether you choose to fill your Easter with indoor crafts, outdoor adventures, or simple home-baked treats, these activities will create lasting memories for all.
