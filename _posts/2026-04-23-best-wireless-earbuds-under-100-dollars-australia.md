## Introduction

In today's fast-paced world, staying connected while on-the-go is more important than ever. For Australians who are always on the move, investing in a pair of high-quality wireless earbuds can significantly enhance your listening experience whether you're commuting or working out.

This article aims to provide a comprehensive guide for Australians looking for the best wireless earbuds under $100. We will explore key features and recommend some top picks that won't break the bank but still deliver excellent sound quality, battery life, and comfort.

## Key Features to Consider

Before diving into our product recommendations, let's take a look at some essential features you should consider when choosing wireless earbuds under $100:

### 1. **Battery Life**
Adequate battery life is crucial for uninterrupted listening sessions. Look for earbuds with at least 4-6 hours of playback time on a single charge.

### 2. **Noise Cancellation**
This feature can be particularly useful if you need to block out ambient noise, such as during your morning jog or in a bustling café.

### 3. **Comfort and Fit**
Earbuds should fit comfortably and securely to prevent them from falling out during physical activity. Consider the size of ear tips available and try different options to find the best fit.

### 4. **Controls and Compatibility**
Ease of use is important, so look for earbuds with intuitive controls such as touch-sensitive surfaces or a dedicated button. Also, ensure they are compatible with your devices (iPhone, Android, etc.).
