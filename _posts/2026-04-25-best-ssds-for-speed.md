## Understanding SSD Speed

Before diving into product reviews, it’s essential to understand what makes an SSD fast. Several factors contribute to an SSD's performance:

### 1. **Sequential Read/Write Speed**
This measures how quickly data can be read from or written to the drive in a continuous stream. Higher sequential speeds mean faster boot times and quicker transfer of large files.

### 2. **Random Access Performance**
Unlike sequential speeds, random access speed tests how fast an SSD can handle multiple small reads and writes simultaneously. This is particularly important for multitasking and running applications efficiently.

### 3. **NAND Flash Technology**
The type of NAND flash memory used by the SSD also affects its performance. TLC (Triple-Level Cell) offers a good balance between cost and speed, while QLC (Quad-Level Cell) provides better value but with slightly reduced speeds compared to TLC.
