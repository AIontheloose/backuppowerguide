## What to Look for in a Budget Laptop

Before diving into specific recommendations, let's discuss what to prioritize when shopping for a budget laptop:

### Processor Performance
Look for at least an Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3 processor. These offer sufficient performance for multitasking and basic productivity applications.

### RAM and Storage
8GB of RAM is the minimum requirement for smooth operation. For storage, a 256GB SSD provides the best balance of speed and capacity for most users.

### Display Quality
A 1080p screen with good brightness and color accuracy is ideal for both work and entertainment.

### Battery Life
At least 6-8 hours of battery life ensures you can work without constantly searching for power outlets.

### Build Quality
While budget laptops may not be as premium as their expensive counterparts, they should still feel sturdy and reliable.
