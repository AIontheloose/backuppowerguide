## Beginner-Friendly Camp Recipes

### 1. One-Pot Pasta with Vegetables

This is the perfect starter recipe for anyone new to camp cooking. The beauty of this dish is its simplicity – you can cook everything in one pot.

**Ingredients:**
- 200g pasta  
- 1 tin of crushed tomatoes  
- 1 red onion, diced  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 zucchini, diced  
- 1 capsicum, diced  
- Olive oil  
- Salt and pepper  
- Fresh basil (optional)  

**Instructions:**
1. Boil pasta in a pot with salt  
2. While pasta cooks, sauté onion and garlic in oil until soft  
3. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes  
4. Add tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes  
5. Drain pasta and mix with sauce  
6. Serve with fresh basil

### 2. Campfire Baked Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are perfect for camping because they're versatile, nutritious, and easy to cook over an open fire.

**Ingredients:**
- 4 sweet potatoes  
- 2 tablespoons butter  
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar  
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon  
- Salt to taste  

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat your campfire or use a portable stove  
2. Poke holes in sweet potatoes with a fork  
3. Wrap in foil and place on hot coals or in oven  
4. Cook for 30-40 minutes  
5. Mix butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon  
6. Remove from heat, slice open, and top with the mixture  

---

### 3. Simple Trail Mix and Cheese Wraps

This recipe is perfect for when you're short on time or ingredients.

**Ingredients:**
- 4 tortillas or wraps  
- 100g cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)  
- Trail mix (nuts, dried fruits, chocolate chips)  
- 1 avocado, sliced (optional)  
- 1 tomato, sliced (optional)  

**Instructions:**
1. Warm tortillas slightly over the fire or on a hot plate  
2. Layer cheese, trail mix, and optional vegetables  
3. Roll up tightly  
4. Cut in half for easy eating  

---

### 4. Campfire Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are a camping staple – they're protein-rich, easy to cook, and travel well.

**Ingredients:**
- 4 eggs  
- 2 tablespoons butter  
- Salt and pepper  
- Optional: cheese, ham, or vegetables  

**Instructions:**
1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk  
2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat  
3. Pour in egg mixture  
4. Stir gently until set  
5. Season with salt and pepper  
6. Add optional ingredients if desired  

---

### 5. Instant Oatmeal with Toppings

This is a great breakfast option that's both filling and easy to prepare.

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup oats  
- 2 cups water or milk  
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup  
- Toppings: nuts, dried fruits, banana slices  

**Instructions:**
1. Bring water or milk to a boil  
2. Add oats and simmer for 2-3 minutes  
3. Stir in sweetener  
4. Top with your favourite additions

## Australian Camp Cooking Tips

### Choose Your Ingredients Wisely

When camping in Australia, consider the local produce available. Fresh fruits like mangoes, papayas, and passionfruit are abundant in summer. Stock up on ingredients that don't spoil quickly and are easy to transport.

### Keep It Simple

Don't overcomplicate your meals. Focus on a few quality ingredients rather than trying to make a feast. This approach reduces waste, saves fuel, and ensures you'll enjoy your food.

### Plan Your Meals

Before you head out, plan your meals for the trip. This helps you pack efficiently and ensures you have all necessary ingredients.

### Safety First

Always follow fire safety guidelines when cooking over an open flame. Keep a fire extinguisher or water nearby, and never leave a fire unattended.

Remember that camp cooking is about more than just the food – it's about the experience. Take time to enjoy the process, involve your camping companions, and don't be afraid to experiment. The key is to have fun while learning new skills.
