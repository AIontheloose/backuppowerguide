## 🍳 Kitchen Deep Cleaning

The kitchen requires special attention due to grease and food residue. Start by removing all items from countertops and cabinets. Clean your stove top thoroughly, removing any burnt-on food or grease. Use a degreaser like **Method Heavy Duty Degreaser** to tackle stubborn kitchen grime.

### 🧼 Deep Clean Checklist:
- Clean your refrigerator from top to bottom
- Remove all food items and wipe down shelves and drawers
- Pay attention to door seals where bacteria can accumulate
- Clean your sink and taps with a bleach solution to eliminate bacteria

### 🧴 Australian-Specific Tips:
For Australian kitchens with granite or benchtops, use a pH-neutral cleaner to avoid damaging surfaces. The **Mrs. Meyers Clean Day** range is perfect for Australian households, offering natural ingredients that are gentle yet effective.

## 🛁 Bathroom Deep Cleaning

Bathrooms are notorious for mould and mildew, especially in Australia's humid climate. Start by removing all towels, toiletries, and accessories. Clean your bathtub and shower using a mould remover like **Mold Armor** or **Lysol Bathroom Cleaner**.

### 🧽 Cleaning Steps:
- Scrub tiles and grout with a mixture of baking soda and water
- Use a commercial bathroom cleaner for tougher stains
- Clean your toilet bowl with a toilet brush and disinfectant
- Clean your sink and taps thoroughly, removing limescale build-up

### 🧴 For Hard Water Areas:
Australian homes often have hard water issues, so consider using a lime scale remover like **CLR Calcium, Lime & Rust Remover** to keep your bathroom sparkling.

## 🛏️ Bedroom Deep Cleaning

Your bedroom should be a peaceful sanctuary, so clean it thoroughly. Remove all bedding and wash it in hot water to kill dust mites. Vacuum your mattress thoroughly, using a mattress protector to maintain cleanliness.

### 🧹 Bedroom Tasks:
- Clean your wardrobe and drawers, removing items you no longer need
- Use a vacuum with a crevice tool to clean between furniture and under beds
- Don't forget to dust light fixtures and ceiling fans

### 🧴 Recommended Product:
For a complete bedroom clean, consider using a **Bissell CrossWave** cleaning system that can tackle both carpets and hard floors effectively.

## 🧺 Laundry Room Deep Cleaning

The laundry room often gets overlooked but requires regular deep cleaning. Clean your washing machine by running a hot cycle with vinegar or a commercial washing machine cleaner. Clean your dryer vent and filter to prevent fire hazards and improve efficiency.

### 🧹 Organize & Clean:
- Organise your laundry supplies and clean the surrounding area
- Wipe down surfaces and clean any spills or stains immediately

---

## ✅ Final Tips for Australian Homes

In Australia's climate, consider using a dehumidifier during humid months to prevent mould growth. Keep a supply of microfibre cloths and cleaning solutions readily available.

Invest in a quality cleaning kit that includes all essential supplies. The **Bostitch Professional Cleaning Kit** offers everything you need for thorough deep cleaning.

Remember to clean regularly rather than waiting for deep cleaning day. Daily maintenance prevents the build-up of dirt and grime that makes deep cleaning so daunting.

### 🌿 Eco-Friendly Options:
For those looking for green alternatives, try:
- White vinegar + water for general cleaning
- Baking soda for scrubbing tough spots
- Essential oils for natural deodorizing
