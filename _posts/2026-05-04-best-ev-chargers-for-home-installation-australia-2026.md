## Why Level 2 is the Right Choice for Australian Homes

While Level 1 charging (using a standard 120V outlet) is convenient for occasional use, Level 2 charging—operating at 240V—is the preferred solution for home installations. It delivers 10–60 km of range per hour, making it ideal for overnight charging and regular use.

Australia’s electrical infrastructure supports Level 2 installations, though most homes may require an upgrade. A licensed electrician should assess your system before installation to ensure safety and compatibility.

## Australian Market Considerations

### Electrical Infrastructure
Most Australian homes need an electrical upgrade to support Level 2 charging. An electrician should evaluate your home’s capacity to avoid overloading.

### Power Tariffs
Off-peak electricity rates can significantly reduce charging costs. Check with your provider for time-of-use tariffs.

### Weather Protection
Choose chargers with at least IP65 rating to withstand Australia’s diverse climate conditions.
