## Why Choose a Standby Generator?

A standby generator provides automatic power backup when your main electricity supply fails. Unlike portable units, standby generators are permanently installed and automatically activate when power is lost.

**Benefits include:**
- Automatic start-up during outages
- No manual intervention required
- Protection for essential appliances
- Peace of mind during emergencies

In Australia, where weather-related blackouts are common, a standby generator ensures your home remains safe, comfortable, and fully functional.

## Key Considerations for Australian Homeowners

### 1. Fuel Type Selection

Australian homeowners have several fuel options for standby generators:

- **Natural Gas:** Most popular due to convenience and lower ongoing costs.
- **Propane:** Ideal for rural areas without natural gas infrastructure.
- **Diesel:** Offers maximum power output and fuel availability.

### 2. Size and Power Requirements

Most Australian households require between **8,000 and 16,000 watts** of continuous power. Consider:
- Refrigerators
- HVAC systems
- Water heaters
- Lighting and essential appliances

### 3. Installation Requirements

All standby generators require **professional installation**, including:
- Electrical connections
- Fuel line installation
- Ventilation setup
- Transfer switch installation

Ensure your installer is certified and familiar with Australian electrical standards.

### 4. Maintenance and Warranty

Regular maintenance is essential for longevity. Most manufacturers offer:
- 2–5 year warranties
- Some premium models provide up to **10 years** of coverage

Schedule annual maintenance checks to ensure optimal performance.
