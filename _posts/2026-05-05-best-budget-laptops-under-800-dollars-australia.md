# Best Budget Laptops Under 800 Dollars in Australia – Top Picks for 2024

Finding a reliable laptop under $800 in Australia can feel like a needle-in-a-haystack, but the right research makes it doable. ## What to Look For

When shopping for a budget laptop in Australia, focus on these key features:

- **Processor**: Look for at least an Intel Core i3 or AMD Ryzen 3. Avoid older generations for better performance.
- **Memory**: 8GB RAM is the minimum. 16GB is better if you plan to multitask.
- **Storage**: Opt for an SSD (preferably 256GB or more) for faster boot times and better responsiveness.
- **Display**: A 1080p screen with good brightness and viewing angles is ideal for most users.
