Data has evolved from a tech novelty into a genuine wellness tool. In 2026, tracking sleep quality is just as critical as logging mileage. Poor recovery derails progress faster than a missed workout. ### Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I really need a coach to run effectively?**
While self-guided running works for some, a coach provides periodization, accountability, and real-time adjustments based on your feedback. This prevents the common trap of "gray zone" training and helps you peak at the right time while minimizing injury risk.

**What if I miss a workout or have a bad day?**
Consistency over perfection. If you miss a session, swap it with a cross-training day or skip it without guilt. Your coach will adjust the load so you don't fall behind or burn out. The plan is a framework, not a rigid contract.
