## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When’s the best time to buy tech gifts in Australia before Christmas?**  
A: Early November offers the strongest balance of stock availability and seasonal discounts. Major Australian retailers typically roll out their first wave of festive promotions around Black Friday (late November), but booking at least 14 days before 24 December guarantees delivery cuts aren’t missed during peak courier volumes.

**Q: Do Australian warranties cover overseas purchases?**  
A: Not automatically. Always verify “AU stock” or “Australian warranty” labels on product pages. Items shipped from international warehouses often carry manufacturer-only coverage that requires returning devices abroad, which can complicate claims right before the holidays.

**Q: How do I ensure fast delivery during the festive rush?**  
A: Choose local fulfilment centres and select tracked express shipping. Many retailers publish December cut-off dates for standard delivery, so upgrading to courier tracking or opting in-store pickup saves last-minute stress.

**Q: Are there GST-compliant options for corporate gifting?**  
A: Yes. Most major Australian electronics retailers automatically generate tax invoices with clear GST breakdowns upon checkout. Save your order confirmation and receipt emails for end-of-financial-year expense reconciliation or payroll gift card matching.
